Project 3: Digital Video

Introduction and Learning Objective

From this video, I hope that viewers consider the change that working from home has been for many of our pets. I also hope that they employ some of the methods I mention in my video for making their pets happy and content during the workday. Objective: Get others to make their pets happier and less disruptive while they work.

Techniques

I combined closeup shots and full shots when shooting my video, which was mainly to get close and offset the low-light as much as possible. I also had a couple ground-level shots that used the desktop as the ground and brought the viewer to Tinker's level. For movement, I used 2 pan shots to incorporate more character into the video, rather than just using all static shots. I included a push transition, a dissolve, and a split. I almost included a dutch angle, but I remembered Thor 2 and thought better of it.

Reflection

I like my video a lot, and I think it does a good job of taking on a sad problem in a good way. Nobody wants to think about sad pets, but addressing it directly with a bit of humor will let viewers/learners consider it without feeling attacked. I think this fits well into my project because it is somewhat useful, but also not totally devoted to being strictly about utility.
