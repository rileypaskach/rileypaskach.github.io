Project 2: Audio

Link to my audio clip

Introduction and Learning Objective

From this audio clip, students should fully understand what the purpose of the site is, and whether or not they may be interested in the rest of the content.

Licensing

The song I used for the background music is "Find Your Way Beat" by Nana Kwabena and the license attached to it is the YouTube Audio Library License, which means it can be freely used in videos without credit, even for monetization.

Techniques

  • Used a filter to raise the pitch of my voice for the proposed question at the start of the video
  • Captured a noise print of the background static to use for noise reduction
  • Audio file fades in and out
  • Includes background music and voice track between 30 and 90 seconds

Reflection

I think my audio clip does a good job of establishing the over-arching theme of the site. It grabs a student/site visitor and connects a potential problem in their lives with a solution. My voiceover was clear, especially after adding a noice reduction filter.
