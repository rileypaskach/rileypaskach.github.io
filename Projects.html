Complete!

These are the projects I have completed and used to help myself or someone else save time or accomplish something that wasn't otherwise possible.

  • Bulk Keyword Isolater

    This script can reference a list of words and remove them from another, larger list of words.

    Use Case: SEMRush allows you to pull 30k keywords that your competitors are bidding on. If you wanted to see how many of those keywords contain brands that your client also sells, that could prove to be a difficult task in excel. This script allows you to add 1 csv of all your brands and instantly create a list of keywords that overlap.

  • Ad Account Visual

    This visual, built with Python, is meant to illustrate the hierarchy of an ad account with nested circles. Ad groups are nested within campaigns and all are sized based on a relative value of your choosing.

    Use Case: Quickly see which ad groups are absorbing the most budget, and cross-reference with a clear visual of what ad groups are bringing in the most conversions.

  • Excel Formatter

    Takes a CSV that has to be pulled every week and automatically formats it to be client-ready. This can be done on multiple CSVs at once.

    Use Case Any report that generates fields with identical names week over week can eaily be automated to reduce formatting time. This script saves time spent on simple formatting and can get people some time back to focus on more complicated tasks.
